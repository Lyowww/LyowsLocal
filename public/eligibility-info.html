✅ Conditions for Eligibility

To be eligible for future airdrops, you must meet the following three conditions:

1. Reach Level 3

Complete quests and increase your passive income to level up and meet this requirement.

2. Make a TON Transaction

Validate your wallet by completing at least one TON transaction.

3. Invite 3 Friends

Share your affiliate link with friends, groups, or others to fulfill this condition. Alternatively, if you prefer not to invite friends, you can purchase “friends” in the shop to validate this requirement.

✅ Eligibility Criteria

Several factors significantly boost your chances of winning. Each criterion earns you points, increasing your probability of being selected:

1. Points Accumulated Since the Start of the Game

The more points you’ve earned, the higher your chances of being selected.

2. Number of Quests Completed

Every completed quest counts. Completing more quests increases your chances of winning.

3. Number of Friends Invited

Inviting more friends earns you more points, which boosts your chances of receiving an airdrop.

✅ Bonuses

Bonuses can significantly multiply your chances of winning one or more airdrops. There are two types of bonuses available:

1. NFTs

Users can mint up to 5 NFTs directly from their profile by connecting their wallet.

Each minted NFT grants a 100% bonus to your chances of receiving an airdrop. If you hold 5/5 NFTs, your bonus increases by an additional 100%.

2. $JOK Holders

If you hold JOK Tokens, you receive additional bonuses based on your grade: (Paste your ERC20 address in profile if you are on ETH network or connect TON keeper if you are on TON network)

Grade Number of JOK

3/5 5M JOK

Holding $JOK provides a 100% bonus for each grade to your airdrop chances, with an additional 100% bonus for holding the maximum grade (5/5).
